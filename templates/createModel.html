Ordinary least squares (OLS)

Useful for analysing sensor data in IoT devices. For example, it can help predict energy consumption in smart homes based on past usage patterns.

Lasso

Useful for filtering important information from large volumes of IoT data. For example, it can identify sensor faults to anticipate industrial equipment failures.

SVM Classification

Useful for categorising IoT data, such as differentiating between normal and abnormal operation of machines in a smart factory based on sensor readings.

SVM Regression

Useful for predicting future trends in IoT, such as estimating the lifetime of components in smart devices from wear and tear data.

KMeans

Useful for segmenting large IoT data sets. For example, it can group users in a smart city according to their water or electricity consumption patterns, helping in the optimisation of resources.
